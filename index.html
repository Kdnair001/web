What to Expect in First Semester

As a computer engineering student, you will explore a variety of topics, To help you get started, here are the list of the subjects with the syllabus you are going to study:

  • Communication Skills in English
  • Mathematics I
  • Applied Physics I
  • Applied Chemistry
  • Engineering Graphics
  • Applied Physics Lab
  • Applied Chemistry Lab
  • Introduction to IT systems Lab
  • Engineering Workshop Practice
  • Sports and Yoga

Be prepared for a rigorous but rewarding journey filled with challenging projects and collaborative experiences.
